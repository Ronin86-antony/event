Key Features

Event Listings

Organizers can easily list, update, and manage events through an intuitive dashboard with automatic categorization.

Search & Filter

Participants can explore events using filters like location, date, interest area, and event type for better discoverability.

Real-Time Updates

Event details are updated in real-time to ensure accuracy and avoid discrepancies. Get notified of any changes.

Open Calendar Format

Supports open calendar formats (RSS/Atom feeds and .ics file) to help participants track upcoming events.

Community Interaction

Discussion forums allow participants to collaborate and network before, during, and after events.

Social Media Integration

Events can be easily shared on social media platforms for wider outreach and increased visibility.

About Kerala TechConnect

Kerala TechConnect is your gateway to the vibrant tech ecosystem across Kerala's college communities. Our platform serves as a centralized hub for discovering, organizing, and participating in technology events that shape the future of innovation in Kerala.

Our mission is to connect students, developers, organizations, and tech enthusiasts, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing through various events including hackathons, workshops, meetups, and conferences.
